KEBAP

Ingredients

  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or lamb
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:

    In a large bowl, combine the ground meat, chopped onion, minced garlic, spices, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined. You can add chopped parsley for extra flavor.
